Anthony Mixed Fruit Juice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 orange (peeled and segmented)
  • 1 apple (chopped, seeds removed)
  • Pineapple (chopped)
  • Mango (peeled and chopped)

For the Filling

  • Cup cold water or coconut water
  • teaspoon honey or suger (optional)
  • A few ice cubes (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all fruits into small pieces.
  2. Add the fruits to a blander.
  3. Pour in the water (or coconut water)
  4. Blend untill smooth.
  5. Strain the juice using a sieve or cheesecloth (optional if you prefer pulp-free juice)
  6. Taste and adjust sweetness with honey or sugar if needed.
  7. Server chilled, with ice cubes and mint leaves for freshness
